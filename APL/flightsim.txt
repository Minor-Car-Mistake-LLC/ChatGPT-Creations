∇forces←computeForces(v,α,ρ,S,Cd0,Cdα,Cla,Clmax)
⍝ Compute lift and drag forces on an aircraft
⍝ Inputs:
⍝  v: velocity (m/s)
⍝  α: angle of attack (rad)
⍝  ρ: air density (kg/m^3)
⍝  S: wing area (m^2)
⍝  Cd0: zero-lift drag coefficient
⍝  Cdα: drag coefficient slope
⍝  Cla: lift coefficient slope
⍝  Clmax: maximum lift coefficient
⍝ Outputs:
⍝  forces[1]: lift force (N)
⍝  forces[2]: drag force (N)
⍝
⍝ Compute lift and drag coefficients
Cl←Cla×α
Cl←Cl×⍨(1+⍣(Clmax÷Cla))⌊Cl÷Clmax
Cd←Cd0+Cdα×Cl×Cl
⍝ Compute forces
L←0.5×ρ×S×v×v×Cl
D←0.5×ρ×S×v×v×Cd
forces←L D
∇
